User research is crucial for product designers as it enables them to gain insight into their target audience's needs, preferences, and behaviors. By conducting user research, designers can create products tailored to the user's needs, preferences, and expectations, resulting in products that are more likely to be adopted and used. Ultimately, investing in user research can save time and resources in the long run by creating products more likely to succeed in the market. ## Product Design Strategy & Planning


Design strategy and planning are critical for product designers to create successful and impactful products. A well-thought-out design strategy ensures that the product meets the user's needs, aligns with the company's goals and values, and is unique in the market. Planning helps the designer organize their ideas and decide about the product's form, function, and aesthetics. It also allows for the consideration of potential challenges and the development of solutions to mitigate risks. By investing time in design strategy and planning, product designers can increase their chances of creating innovative, effective, and well-received products that meet user and business needs.
