## What is Notion AI?


[Notion AI](https://affiliate.notion.so/notionstack-ai) is a feature that allows you to use artificial intelligence to enhance your productivity within Notion. Similar to Chat GPT, you can ask Notion AI questions. But the true power of Notion AI is the ability to use AI in the context of the Notion workspace to write documents, build tables, identify tasks, and so much more. Notion was one of the first applications to provide AI built into the application, so you expect more features and functionality shortly. In the meantime, to access Notion AI in your Notion workspace, follow the following steps:


**Notion AI Menu**


By simply typing **/AI**, the Notion AI prompt will appear with multiple options, such as changing the tone or translating text. The find action items or summarize features are great for long documents.
