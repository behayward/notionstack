## Notion Website Builder Purchasing Considerations

- **Support** - No-code website building is a huge time saver, but sometimes you will need some help configuring and customizing your website, so access to help documents and support is important. Also, Notion is launching new features weekly, so you want a service that actively keeps up with new Notion blocks launches.
- **Templates** - It is helpful to start the website-building process with a pre-built template vs. a blank page.
- **Use cases** - Some services provide general website support, while others specialize in specific use cases (e.g., documentation, help, change logs).
- **Additional costs** - Most services provide integrations into other services (e.g., form builders and analytics), which come with an additional cost.
