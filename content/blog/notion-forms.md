We have also used Tally to create courses and fairly complex logic-dependent surveys, which all work great. We have also been impressed with the Tally team and the monthly new features and functionality releases.

## Forms Builders That Sync Form Submissions to a Notion Database


If you plan to capture form submissions in a Notion database but outside of Notion or on a [Notion-powered website](/2c1db271662042f89355c849125b8cec), consider a few form building options. The following form builders have native integrations (i.e., don’t require an additional service like Zapier) with Notion:

- [Paperform](https://paperform.co/integrations/notion/)
- [Typeform](https://www.typeform.com/connect/notion/)
- [forms.app](https://forms.app/en/integrations/notion/formsapp)

We are big fans of using forms to engage and better serve our community, so we hope you can do the same with one of the Notion form providers. Please let us know if you have any questions or recommend a service not listed here.
