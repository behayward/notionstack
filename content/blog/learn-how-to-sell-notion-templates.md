## FAQ: Selling Notion Templates


### Does Notion allow users to sell templates?


Notion is designed to be collaborative, which is why Notion makes it super easy to create and [share templates](https://www.notion.so/help/sharing-and-permissions). Click the share options in the upper right corner of your template to obtain a shareable link. Once you sell and share your own templates, the buyer can duplicate the template and will have access to the template going forward.


### Where should I share my templates?


In addition to sharing on social media and with your audience, we recommend adding your templates to sites like NotionStack that will broaden your audience. Creators can [submit their creations to NotionStack](/a6bd8191ba0d4d0e9b49ce405799ede2) to reach a broader audience.


### How much money can you make selling templates?


Some creators have quit their jobs to sell Notion templates full-time, while others are selling Notion templates to supplement their income.


### How do I sell Notion templates?


Most creators post and sell templates on platforms such as [Etsy](https://www.etsy.com/), [Gumroad](https://gumroad.com/), [Whop](https://whop.com/?a=user5194690247), and [Lemon Squeezy](https://www.lemonsqueezy.com/) because it is super easy to promote, accept payments and fulfill template purchases.


### What are some ideas for creating Notion templates?


Check out the templates we have added to [NotionStack](/86f5352f0a834816a04e6d9d5a00be48) to get some ideas and inspiration for your template creation. Some of the most popular templates are database templates, [habit trackers](/c8f9627533ca41458c44a0bacdc6e7f5), task management, [life planners](/af344914c0114f3f940d4ee464cff5b5), [journaling](/a90c27798a0d454a82d464cdf8ec924e) and [personal finance](/fe49bb9d7e35482ca32b39351dbd0735) templates.
